About Midnight Tales Archive

Midnight Tales Archive is a solo-run channel telling original fiction — mystery, suspense, and "what if" stories, built from scratch for YouTube Shorts and TikTok. Every story is original: no stolen footage, no recycled scripts, no real events dressed up as fact. Scripts are written with AI assistance, narrated, and produced independently, then reviewed before anything goes public.

The goal is simple: stories short enough to catch your attention, good enough to make you stay.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: [today's date]

Midnight Tales Archive is a personal content-creation tool used to generate and publish original short-form video content to YouTube and TikTok.

What we collect: This app does not collect, store, or sell personal data from any third party or general public user. What we access: With explicit OAuth consent, the app may access: channel/account identity, upload permissions, and basic analytics (views, engagement) for the connected account only.

Data storage: OAuth tokens are stored locally and securely on the operator's own device. They are never transmitted to or stored on any third-party server beyond the platforms' own official APIs (Google, TikTok).

Data sharing: No data is shared with advertisers, analytics companies, or any other third party.
